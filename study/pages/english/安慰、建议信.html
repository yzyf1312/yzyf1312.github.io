安慰、建议信

开头句式

  1. [Knowing/Learning] that you have trouble in... after reading your letter, I would like to offer some suggestions which may be helpful to you.

  2. I am glad to receive your letter asking for my advice [on/with regard to/regarding]... and I' ll try to make some useful suggestions here.

中间句式

  1. It would be [a good idea/a better choice] to do

  2. It would be more beneficial if you could do

  3. It is [important/vital/significant/wise/advisable] to do

  4. It is high time that sb [did/should do] sth.

  5. When it comes to such a situation, I would like to suggest that you should

  6. Another effecitive suggestion is

  7. Doing sth is also highly recommended.
